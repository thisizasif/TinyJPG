TinyJPG — Fast Image Compression in Your Browser

TinyJPG helps you reduce image file size without sacrificing visual quality. Set a target size, compress multiple files at once, and download them instantly — all locally in your browser.

Why TinyJPG?

  • Private, in‑browser compression
  • Target size controls in KB/MB/KiB/MiB
  • Batch processing with ZIP download
  • JPG, PNG, and WebP support

Common Questions

Does TinyJPG upload my images?

No. Everything stays in your browser.

How accurate is target sizing?

TinyJPG automatically balances quality and resizing to reach your goal. If the target can’t be reached, it will show a note.

Can I compress many images at once?

Yes. Add multiple files and compress them in a single batch.
